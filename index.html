Top News Feed bookmarklet

Find the best posts in your News Feed. Just drag this bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar:

Top News Feed

Best posts at the top

Too much boring content in your Facebook/Twitter/Google+ News Feed? Top News Feed helps you find the best posts in the News Feed and Profiles, sorted by likes. No server-side processing involved, no privacy concerns!

How to use it

Drag the Top News Feed bookmarklet above to your bookmarks bar, then go to any Facebook/Twitter/Google+/Instagram profile and run it. By clicking the settings icon on the right, you can choose how many News Feed pages will be requested.
